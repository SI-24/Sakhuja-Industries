DEFENCE & STRATEGIC ELECTRONICS

Enabling India's Indigenous Defence Technology Base.

SIPL enables the development and deployment of indigenous defence and strategic electronics — through partnerships, subsystem integration, commercialization support, and deployment readiness across sensing, monitoring, and mission-critical systems.

Our role: SIPL is an enabler and integrator, not a weapons designer or manufacturer. We work alongside research institutions, OEMs, and manufacturing partners to take validated electronics and subsystems from prototype to deployment-ready capability.
