About Us

A Design-First
Electronics Company

We operate at the confluence of hardware innovation and manufacturing strategy — building India's design capability from the ground up.

Sakhuja Industries Pvt. Ltd. is a design-focused electronics company dedicated to building deep capabilities in PCB design and embedded electronics.

Our work is driven by a clear strategic intent: to help India move from being an assembly-first economy to a design-led manufacturing powerhouse — where intellectual property, engineering talent, and fabrication strength coexist in one ecosystem.

Every board we design, every firmware module we write, and every system we architect is a step toward a future where Indian engineers define the global electronics landscape.

🔬

Precision Engineering

High-density PCB layouts, impedance-controlled routing, and DFM outputs that meet global standards.

🧠

Embedded Intelligence

Firmware and embedded software that turn silicon into intelligent, responsive systems across industrial and consumer domains.

🇮🇳

Strategic National Vision

Aligned with India's semiconductor mission — building local design IP rather than only assembling imported boards.

Mission Statement

To establish Sakhuja Industries as a design-led electronics entity that contributes original engineering capability to India's manufacturing ecosystem — bridging the gap between design intent and production-ready hardware.

Design Capabilities

Our Technology
Stack & Skills

A growing portfolio of hardware design competencies, supported by industry-standard EDA tools and a disciplined methodology from concept to production.

Core Services

What We Design & Build

From schematic capture to production-ready output, our capabilities span the full hardware development lifecycle.

🔲

PCB Design & Layout

Multi-layer PCB design with controlled impedance, differential pair routing, and power integrity analysis. From 2-layer to 12-layer high-speed HDI designs.

⚡

Schematic Capture & BOM

Structured schematic design with hierarchical sheets, component library management, and intelligent BOM generation tied directly to design data.

💾

Embedded Firmware

Bare-metal C/C++ and RTOS firmware for ARM Cortex-M, ESP32, and RISC-V. Protocol stacks: I²C, SPI, UART, CAN, USB, BLE/WiFi.

🛡️

Signal & Power Integrity

Pre-layout simulation and post-layout verification ensuring signal quality, EMI compliance, and power delivery network stability.

🏭

DFM & Fabrication Readiness

Design rule checks aligned to JLCPCB, PCBway, and local Indian fab houses. Complete Gerber, drill, and assembly packages ready for production.

🔭

Prototyping & Bring-Up

Hardware debug, oscilloscope-assisted bring-up, and hardware-firmware co-development from first prototype to production revision.
